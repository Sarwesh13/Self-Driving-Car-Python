In main.py,import and create instances of the Car, Road, TrafficCar, and other necessary classes, and manage the game loop.
In car.py, define the player's car behavior.
In road.py, handle drawing the road.
In traffic.py, manage traffic cars, their behavior, and collision detection.
In controls.py, handle user input.


For collision detection pygame actually has a method called 'colliderect' which detects collision between rectangles
